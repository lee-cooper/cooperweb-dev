Hello, my Name is Lee Cooper

Accessibility

The Web is for everyone, which is why I work to ensure that the sites and pages I create are accessible and WCAG 2.1 compliant, while leveraging my skills to help others learn and implement solutions that align with these standards.

"The power of the Web is in its universality. Access by everyone regardless of disability is an essential aspect."

- Tim Berners-Lee, W3C Director and inventor of the World Wide Web

ABOUT

Who Am I?

Hello! My name is Lee Cooper. I'm a web developer with experience developing, deploying, and maintaining enterprise-level web applications, with a specialization in designing accessible and user-friendly interfaces and components.

Background

I was introduced to software development during my second year of college, where I took an "Introduction to C++" course as an elective while I finished out the remainder of my classes for my business associates degree.

After the first class, I was hooked.

Switching my major, I went on to achieve my bachelors degree in Information Systems Technology at Southern Illinois University in Carbondale and accepted a position at Progressive Insurance as an IT Applications Developer.

That one class offering began my journey of becoming a professional software developer, where I get to do what I love everyday - build software that changes the lives of others.

What Am I Up To Now?

In addition to my work at Progressive, my 2022 goals are to teach web development classes and create websites for local businesses in need of a web presence. This led to the creation of cooperweb.dev as a way to reach these goals.

Explore the site, and feel free to reach out.

ABOUT

Lee Cooper

Who am I?

I'm a web developer with 5 years of professional experience developing, deploying, and maintaining enterprise-level applications, with a specialization in designing accessible and user-friendly interfaces and components.

Background

I was introduced to software development during my 2nd year of college, where I took an "Introduction to C++" course as an elective while I finished out the remainder of my classes for my business associates degree.

After the first class, I was hooked.

I pivoted, dropping my business degree and changed my major and courseload with classes related to software and web development (thus adding an extra year of college), achieved my bachelors degree in Information Systems Technology, and landed a position at a Fortune 100 company as an IT Applications Developer.

That one class offering began my journey into becoming a professional software developer, where I get to to do what I love everyday - built software that changes the lives of others.

What Am I Up to Now?

In addition to my 9-5 work, I am also branching out into teaching and freelancing work, specializing in web design and development. This led to the creation of this site, cooperweb.dev to provide a means to achieve these goals.

Explore the site, and feel free to contact me through my site's contact form for any questions or work opportunities.
